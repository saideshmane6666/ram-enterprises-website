Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What documents do I need for insurance?

Answer: Documents required vary by type of insurance. For life insurance, typically PAN card and identity proof are needed. For motor insurance, you'll need vehicle registration and driving license. For health insurance, age proof and medical history may be required. Our team will guide you through the exact documents needed for your specific policy.

❓ How long does the policy issuance take?

Answer: We offer quick policy issuance. Life and health insurance policies are typically issued within 2-3 business days after documentation. Motor insurance can be issued same-day. FASTag can be registered and activated within 24 hours.

❓ What is the insurance claim process?

Answer: Our claims process is simple and hassle-free: (1) Notify us of the claim, (2) Submit required documents, (3) Our team processes the claim, (4) Settlement is made based on policy terms. We provide 24/7 support throughout the process.

❓ How does FASTag work?

Answer: FASTag is a device that uses RFID technology for automatic toll collection. Mount it on your vehicle's windshield, link it to a bank account, and pass through toll plazas without stopping. The toll fee is automatically deducted. We provide registration, recharge, and renewal support.

❓ What can GPS Tracking do?

Answer: Our GPS Tracking system provides real-time vehicle location, anti-theft alerts, speed monitoring, geofencing, and detailed trip analytics. It's ideal for personal vehicle safety or commercial fleet management. You can track your vehicle 24/7 via our mobile app.

❓ Do I get discount on premiums?

Answer: Yes! We offer various discounts based on policy type, coverage period, and claim-free history. Additionally, many insurance companies offer cashback or discounts through us. Our team can help identify all applicable discounts for your situation.

❓ Can I renew my existing policy?

Answer: Absolutely! We handle renewals for all types of insurance. You can renew online or contact us directly. We'll ensure timely renewal so there's no gap in your coverage. Early renewal often comes with additional benefits.

❓ What are your working hours?

Answer: We're available 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM every day (Monday to Sunday). You can reach us by phone, email, or WhatsApp during these hours. Outside working hours, you can still submit inquiries and we'll respond the next available time.
